Voice

We conducted a questionnaire to find out how many people in Japan are currently feeling stressed and how they are coping with stress.
※A total of 165 people, 97 people under 18 years old (mainly junior and senior high school students) and 68 adults, answered the questionnaire.

Question List

  • ① Have you been feeling stressed recently?

    • (If you answered "yes" to question ① below.)

  • ② Do you think you feel stressed easily?
  • ③ Have you ever been sick due to stress?
  • ④ What kind of things make you feel stressed?
  • ⑤ Please tell us your stress relief method.

①Have you been feeling stressed lately?

Although the degree of stress felt may vary from person to person, more than 70% of both children and adults answered that they feel stressed.

②Do you think you feel stressed easily?

The results showed that Children are more likely to think that they are easily stressed.

③Have you ever gotten sick from stress?

The results showed that around 60% of both children and adults have experienced stress-related health problems. It is clear that stress and the body are closely related.

④What are some of the things that stress you out?

We found that many of the respondents were stressed out by their relationships and anxiety.
Others included work and study.

⑤What is your stress relief method?

The most common response was "sleeping", followed by "singing", "listening to music", and "playing games". Others included "Spending time alone", "Thinking of stress as an opportunity to grow or to pretend it never happened". "Having several options available for stress."

From the results of this survey, we can see that there are many people who are stressed, who feel stress easily, and who have gotten sick due to stress.
It is difficult to avoid feeling stress, but I felt the need to prevent and deal with stress. Knowing these things will be a step toward making your mind and body more resistant to stress.
