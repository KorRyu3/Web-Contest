Stress check

Let's check your state of mind!
Please think "Yes" or "No" to the following questions.

Q1. Do you feel depressed or unmotivated?

Yes

You may be stressed.If the symptoms continue, don't put up with it andtalk to your family or a specialized institution.

No

That sounds great! However, the symptoms of stress vary, so if you feelsomething is wrong, let's rest without patience.

For more imformation

External site (厚生労働省 こころもメンテしよう -ストレスのサイン-)

Q2. Do you feel sick with headaches,abdominal pain, stiff shoulders, etc.?

Yes

There is a possibility of some kind of illness, but if you go to the hospital,you don't know the cause, you may be stressed and your body is saying SOS.Let's rest our mind and body without patience.

No

That sounds great! Let's continue to maintain physical health.

For more imformation

External site (厚生労働省 こころもメンテしよう -ストレスのサイン-)

Q3. Do you suddenly feel intense anxiety, palpitations or dizziness for no reason?

Yes

A disease that causes the above symptoms, as well as recurring sudden symptomssuch as weating, shaking, and nausea, is called "panic disorder".If the symptoms continue, don't put up with it and talk to your family or a specialized institution.

No

That sounds great! Let's continue to take care of yourself and maintain our health.

For more imformation

External site (厚生労働省 こころもメンテしよう -不安障害-)

Q4. Do you exercise regularly?

Yes

That sounds great! Physical health is related to mental health.Let's keep it going.

No

Exercise is a great way to relieve negative moods and relax your mind.Even simple take a walk around the neighborhood can help your health.

For more imformation

External site (厚生労働省 こころもメンテしよう -体を動かす-)

Q5. Do you eat a well-balanced diet?

Yes

That sounds great! Let's continue your habit.

No

A well-balanced diet and a regular lifestyle can make your body resistant to stress.Let's review your lifestyle.
