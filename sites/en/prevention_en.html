Prevention

Three rules to overcome stress!

Living a regular life

In order to build a body that can prevent stress, living a regular life is the highest priority. The reason is that stress attacks the weakest parts of the human body.
For example, people with a weak stomach and intestines develop gastrointestinal symptoms such as gastric ulcer.
A healthy life, such as a balanced diet, moderate exercise, and good sleep, helps prevent fatigue and is the first step in building a healthy body that can withstand stress.

Laughing

Laughing is said to be good for your physical and mental health. When you laugh, your sympathetic nerves dominate and your blood vessels contract. Then, the parasympathetic nerves become active and a relaxing effect is created.

When you feel tired or depressed, turn to what you like and bring laughter into your life. If you think you haven't enjoyed it lately, why don't you find a new hobby?

Playing

"Playing" means having a special experience and adding some changes in your daily lives. An everyday life without changes means no stimulation, witch becomes vulnerable to stress.

It dose not mean playing every day, but it's important to play occasionally (on holidays) to get positive inspiration and cut off negative thoughts. people often think about it on weekends when they have anxiety or worry at school or work. However, try not to think about it on weekends and do what you can't do on weekdays.
