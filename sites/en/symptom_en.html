Symptom

Stressors affect the autonomic nervous system and endocrine system, causing a variety of symptoms. Therefore, the symptoms that appear vary from person to person.

Early symptoms of stress

  • Getting tired easily, Not recovering from fatigue
  • Having an stomach, Stomachache
  • Headache
  • Body pains


The stressor will attack your weaknesses. It's hard to notice that you are getting stressed in the early stages, but knowing your poor physical condition early can help you deal with it better.
The longer you feel stressed, the worse your symptoms will be, witch leads to psychosomatic disorders and adjustment disorders.

Illness

There are many kind of illnesses that can be caused or exacerbated by stress. Here are some of the most famous. The names and symptoms of stress can change over time, and the different names of illnesses can be similar.
Please note that the names and symptoms of the diseases listed below are only a few example.

Psychosomatic disorder

Psychosomatic disorder is the condition when stress causes physical symptoms and illness.
The main symptoms of psychosomatic disorder are shown in the figure at each location of the body.

[contents]

Whole body ： Getting tired easily, Fatigue, Slight fever
Head ： Headache, Tinnitus, Hair loss, Stiff shoulder
Body ： arrhythmia, High blood pressure, Palpitations
Stomach ： Stomach pain, Nausea, Diarrhea

The symptoms witch are categorized by body organs include the following.

Digestive system ： Stomach upset, Stomach pain, Nausea, Diarrhea
Respiratory system ： Difficulty breathing, Chest tightness
Cardiovascular system ： Blood pressure elevation, Dizziness, Tinnitus, Palpitations Arrhythmia
skeletal muscular system ： Numbness in limbs, Headache, Stiff shoulders
Other symptoms ： Hair loss, Slight fever, Eyelid twitches.

These symptoms can occur at the same time.

Depression

Depression dose not occur suddenly, but it is caused by an accumulation of small mental burdens.
It is the most recognized mental illness, but its symptoms and tendencies have diversified in recent years.
It is currently diagnosed by the degree of depression (decrease of mental energy).

The symptoms include persistent depression, loss of interest, and eating and sleeping disorder.
In mild cases, you will manage to go to school or work. It is hard to be noticed because it does not affect an everyday life. When it becomes severe, it is becomes difficult to live a daily life. Negative delusions become more intense, and some people might want to "die".

Adjustment disorder

It is a illness that causes physical and mental symptoms due to stress of changes in the environment, witch leads to obstacles in a daily life.
Since it is illness that can occur to anyone, the prevalence is 5 to 20%. The symptoms vary from mental to physical problems. The characteristic of adjustment disorders is that "there is obvious stress." If this cause is removed, the symptoms will improve relatively quickly.
According to diagnostic criteria, the symptoms improve within 6 months after the cause stress is removed.

Neurosis

Although the name is neurosis, it does not mean that there are actually problems in the nerves.
Neurosis refers to a "mentally debilitated state." Psychosomatic disorder is also classified as one of the neuroses.
The Symptoms include palpitation, dizziness, depression, and lethargy. These symptoms may develop into autonomic imbalance.
The name of neurosis is a very big field. It is subdivided into such as

  • Panic disorder
  • Phobia
  • Social Anxiety Disorder

it is divided into such as.
As the name implies, all illnesses is based on "anxiety" and "fear".
The causes of these symptoms are the following.

  • 1．Personality (perfectionism and nervousness)
  • 2．Past trauma
  • 3．Environment
  • 4．Heredity

Treatment

Treatment for mental illness depends on a hospital policy, symptomatology severity, and patients.
The main focus is on "drug treatment," "lifestyle improvement," and "counseling".
Drug treatment has advantage that the effect comes out faster, but also has disadvantage that symptoms may recur when the drug is stopped.
Early treatment is important for any illness. it is normal to feel sick or depressed sometimes, but if you feel sick frequently, or feel clearly different from usual, feel free to contact your healthcare provider or professional.

Sometimes just talking to someone makes you feel better.
Don't try to deal with it by yourself, and please say to "I'm tired".
